Welcome

This association is a Voluntary Organization doing its activities especially for the betterment of future of the poor and rural women. We are doing our job in identifying, motivating, encouraging and educating those women. Hence we have given a humble start, by taking a building on rent in Pedana to provide effective training programmes for women and youth to bring about a positive change in the society at large through training as a project activity.

The programmes conducted by us for the students are as follows:-

  1. TAILORING AND EMBROIDARY TRAINING TO THE WOMEN AND YOUTH
  2. COMPUTER AND TYPE

We have a special programme called “Vasthra Datha”. We collect old clothes, repair them, clean them and pass them on to the needy people. In our programme “Amrutha” we take ordinary teenagers and train them at various nursing homes to be good bed side nurses, to work for a patient who is bed ridden either at home or at any nursing home.
